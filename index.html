Emma Woodward

There was a play in Lutheran kindergarden based on the parable of casting seeds on fertile ground. Five year old Emma was cast as the prostitute. After the entire class broke out in laughter, Emma bawled her eyes out and another girl offered to take the role. The other girl was a great prostitute and clearly had fun. Emma played a bean. This moment shaped little Emma and she has since enjoyed every role.
